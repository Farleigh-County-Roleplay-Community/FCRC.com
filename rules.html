Server Rules & Regulations

Welcome to Farleigh County Roleplay Community! These rules ensure everyone enjoys a fair, respectful, and immersive experience. All players and members must follow these rules in both Discord and in-game. Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action. Remember: roleplay realism and respect form the foundation of our community.

Game Rules

Rule What It Is Example

Roblox ToSFollow all Roblox rules.Posting NSFW or using exploits.
