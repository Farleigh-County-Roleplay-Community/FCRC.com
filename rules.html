Community Rules

These rules ensure a safe, fair, and enjoyable experience for everyone in Farleigh County Roleplay. Please take the time to read and understand them before participating in any session or activity.

In-Game Rules

Rule What It Is Example
General ConductRespect all players and staff; avoid exploits and glitches.Following staff instructions.
VDMDon't run over players without RP reason.Intentionally hitting people with vehicles.
RDMNo killing without valid RP initiation.Shooting players for no reason.
FRPValue your life in every situation.Pulling a weapon while surrounded.
NLRDon't return to RP scenes after death.Rejoining the same shootout.
PowergamingDon’t force actions on others."/me stabs you and you die instantly."
MetagamingNo OOC information in RP.Using Discord info to locate players.
LTAP / LTAR / LTAADon’t leave to avoid RP, arrest, or punishment.Leaving mid-arrest or during punishment.
Crime RegulationsMajor crimes must follow cooldowns; no terrorism or mass events.Planning mass attacks is not allowed.
Emergency ServicesAct professionally and follow department rules.Abusing department commands or tools.
Roleplay StandardsKeep RP realistic and immersive.Ignoring injuries or refusing to RP.
Scene InterruptionDon’t interfere with ongoing RP scenes.Disrupting active law enforcement roleplays.
Roblox ToSFollow Roblox Terms of Service.No NSFW content or exploits.
EnforcementStaff decisions are final; appeals available.Submitting appeals properly.
