FAQ

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our community, operations, and how to get involved.

How do I join the roleplay sessions?

Join our official Discord server and follow the verification and application steps for access to roleplay sessions.

Is there an age requirement to join?

We recommend members be at least 13 years old in compliance with Discord and Roblox’s Terms of Service.

Do I need to be in a department to roleplay?

No, you can join as a civilian or apply to a department if you’d like to roleplay as law enforcement, fire, EMS, or other roles.

How can I apply for staff or developer positions?

When recruitment is open, applications are announced in our Discord server under the Staff Openings section.

What do I do if I get banned or punished?

You can submit an appeal through our official appeal website, which will be reviewed by the moderation team.

How can I report a player or staff member?

Use the report system in our Discord server. Provide as much evidence as possible — video, screenshots, or logs help speed up the process.

Do you allow partnerships or advertising?

Partnerships are selectively open to servers that meet our community standards. Advertising without approval is not allowed.
