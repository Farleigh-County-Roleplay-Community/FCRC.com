About Us

Discover the vision, mission, and values that make Farleigh County Roleplay Community stand apart.

Our Community

Farleigh County Roleplay Community (FCRC) is more than just a server. It is a vision of realistic, immersive roleplay that combines structure, creativity, and a welcoming environment. We aim to bring players together under shared values of fairness, integrity, and professionalism.

Our Mission

We believe in setting the gold standard for ER:LC roleplay. Our mission is to offer high-quality, structured sessions while maintaining a fun, engaging atmosphere. Every member of FCRC is valued, and every story adds to the county’s legacy.

Our County

Farleigh County is a fictional Canadian county inspired by the landscapes of southeastern Saskatchewan. Its identity gives depth and realism to every roleplay, from bustling towns to open roads and everything in between.
