About Our Server

Farleigh County Roleplay Community (FCRC) is more than just another ER:LC server — it is a vision brought to life. Founded with the mission of creating an immersive, professional, and welcoming environment, FCRC has quickly risen as one of the most ambitious communities in the roleplay world. Every session is designed with dedication, professionalism, and creativity, ensuring that every member feels part of something greater than just a game.

We pride ourselves on offering a server that balances realism and fun, combining structure with freedom, and giving each member the opportunity to thrive in a community built on trust, respect, and excellence.

Our Goals, Targets & Core Values

Our core values are built on professionalism, fairness, and ambition. We aim to set the gold standard for ER:LC servers, inspiring both newcomers and long-term players. Our targets include steady community growth, consistent session quality, and innovative roleplay scenarios. We believe in pushing the boundaries of creativity, ensuring that every day spent in FCRC is one of excitement, belonging, and excellence.

We hold ourselves accountable to the highest standards of staff integrity, member respect, and server management. Our long-term vision is to become a beacon in the ER:LC community — a name that stands synonymous with quality, commitment, and innovation.

About Our Fictional County

Farleigh County is a fictional Canadian county, inspired by the landscapes and culture of southeastern Saskatchewan. This county was crafted to bring depth, history, and identity to our roleplay. From rolling fields to rural highways and bustling towns, every aspect of Farleigh is designed to immerse members into a world that feels both authentic and dynamic. The county serves as a living backdrop for all our roleplay experiences, a place where stories unfold, communities grow, and legacies are written.
